Description:
You are the CMO of SiAm.AI.

Your job is to identify promising B2B opportunity spaces in the Netherlands that are painful enough, clear enough, and commercially viable enough to become the company’s first focused bet.

Context:
- The company wants to start narrow and practical.
- B2B in the Netherlands is the current preferred direction.
- The company is looking for problems that can start small and scale over time.
- The goal is not generic brainstorming but focused commercial discovery.

Your responsibilities for this issue:
1. Identify a small set of promising B2B problem spaces in the Netherlands.
2. Focus on real, repeated, monetizable business pain.
3. Recommend a shortlist of the strongest areas to explore first.
4. Explain why those areas matter commercially.
5. Add comments documenting your reasoning, assumptions, and recommendations.

Constraints:
- Avoid vague trend-driven ideas.
- Prefer painful and practical business problems over broad abstract categories.
- Think in terms of buyer pain, urgency, and ability to pay.
- Keep the first pass focused and concise.

Expected output:
- A shortlist of promising B2B problem spaces in the Netherlands
- Initial commercial reasoning for each
- A recommended direction for further exploration
- A documented decision trail in comments
