Description:
You are the CEO of SiAm.AI.

The first executive cycle has completed. You now have initial outputs from:
- CEO setup thinking
- CTO technical operating model thinking
- CMO B2B opportunity exploration for the Netherlands

Your job now is to act as the company’s decision-maker and convert those executive outputs into one focused company direction.

Objectives:
- review the executive outputs
- determine whether the company has enough clarity to choose a first B2B direction
- select the first company bet if justified
- decide what the next layer of work should be
- keep the company narrow, practical, and executable

Your responsibilities for this issue:
1. Review the outputs produced so far by the CTO and CMO.
2. Summarize the most important takeaways from each executive function.
3. Decide whether there is a sufficiently strong first B2B opportunity for the company to pursue now.
4. If yes:
   - choose one primary company bet
   - explain why it was selected
   - explain why other options were not selected yet
5. Decide whether the company should hire an additional role now, especially UX Designer or another critical role.
6. Create and delegate the next round of executive issues needed to move from opportunity selection toward validation and MVP shaping.
7. Add comments documenting:
   - your synthesis
   - your decision
   - your delegation logic
   - your view of the main risks or uncertainties
8. If the current outputs are still too weak or vague to justify a company bet, do not force a decision. Instead:
   - explain what is missing
   - create narrower follow-up issues
   - keep the company from drifting into premature solutioning

Decision standards:
- Prefer one strong direction over several weak ones.
- Prefer B2B over B2C unless evidence strongly suggests otherwise.
- Prefer painful, monetizable, repeated problems over broad conceptual ideas.
- Prefer focused validation over broad planning.
- Do not allow the company to move into build mode without enough clarity.

Constraints:
- Do not do specialist execution yourself.
- Delegate rather than implement.
- Do not create excessive parallel work.
- Keep the company focused on the smallest number of priorities needed to move forward intelligently.

Expected output:
- A clear CEO synthesis of CTO and CMO outputs
- A decision on whether the company has a first real bet
- A selected first direction, or a clear reason why no selection is justified yet
- A decision on whether an additional hire is needed now
- A new round of delegated issues to move the company forward
- A documented decision trail in comments
