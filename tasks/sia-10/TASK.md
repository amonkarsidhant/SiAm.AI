Create and maintain a technical risk register for the company.

Scope:
- Identify architecture brittleness risks
- Document integration fragility concerns
- Assess scalability assumptions
- Flag security and data risks
- Document delivery timing risks

Purpose: Early risk visibility enables leadership to make informed decisions about technical tradeoffs.
