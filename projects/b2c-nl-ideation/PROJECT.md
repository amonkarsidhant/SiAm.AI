# B2C - Product / Service / Company Ideation

This project explores consumer opportunities in the Netherlands as a secondary stream.

## Purpose

Observe, collect, and evaluate recurring consumer pain points that may become validation-worthy opportunities.

## Strategic Position

This project is secondary to B2B unless evidence strongly suggests a better path.

## Success Criteria

Success means:
- a set of meaningful consumer pains is captured
- weak ideas are filtered out quickly
- only a few strong possibilities remain for future validation
- the project does not distract the company from stronger B2B execution

## Evaluation Lens

Prefer opportunities with:
- repeated frustration
- clear emotional or financial pain
- signs of willingness to pay or recurring usage
- lightweight validation paths
- the potential to test quickly without building too much

## Anti-Goals

Avoid:
- novelty for its own sake
- broad lifestyle app brainstorming
- expensive consumer growth assumptions too early
- treating this stream as equal priority without evidence
