# B2B - Product / Service / Company Ideation

This project identifies and prioritizes strong B2B opportunities in the Netherlands.

## Purpose

Find painful, repeated, monetizable business problems that can be validated quickly and developed into products or services.

## Core Questions

- Which Dutch business segments have painful, repeated operational problems?
- Which of those problems are urgent enough to pay for?
- Which opportunities fit SiAm.AI's capabilities and operating model?
- Which opportunity should become the company’s first focused bet?

## Success Criteria

Success means:
- a shortlist of strong B2B opportunity spaces exists
- opportunities are compared with a clear scoring lens
- one first bet is selected or the reasons for not selecting are explicit
- the output is concrete enough for MVP and validation planning

## Evaluation Lens

Prefer opportunities with:
- clear buyer pain
- repeated workflow friction
- willingness to pay
- reasonable speed to MVP
- strong B2B distribution logic
- enough differentiation room for a small company

## Anti-Goals

Avoid:
- vague “AI startup ideas”
- trend-driven concepts without clear buyer pain
- broad brainstorming without prioritization
- opportunities that require heavy capital or slow enterprise sales from day one
