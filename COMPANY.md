# SiAm.AI

SiAm.AI is an AI-native company built inside Paperclip to identify, validate, and develop practical products, services, and venture ideas.

## Company Thesis

SiAm.AI focuses on real operational and commercial problems that can be validated quickly and turned into sellable value.

The company starts with a Netherlands-first lens and a bias toward B2B opportunities before broader consumer exploration.

## Strategic Priorities

1. **Onboarding**
   - establish operating rhythm
   - define executive roles and reporting lines
   - create initial project structure
   - set decision and delegation standards

2. **B2B Ideation, Netherlands**
   - identify painful, repeated, monetizable business problems
   - shortlist strong opportunity spaces
   - choose one narrow first bet

3. **B2C Ideation, Netherlands**
   - observe consumer pain points
   - keep this as a secondary stream unless evidence strongly supports it

## Operating Model

SiAm.AI runs as a small executive-led company inside Paperclip.

Current executive roles:
- CEO
- CTO
- CMO
- Clawy

Expected operating behavior:
- the CEO owns strategy, prioritization, hiring, and delegation
- the CTO owns technical strategy and MVP delivery logic
- the CMO owns market research, positioning, and commercial framing
- Clawy supports execution, coordination, and follow-through where assigned
- issues drive execution
- comments create the decision trail
- heartbeats keep work moving

## Decision Principles

SiAm.AI should prefer:
- real pain over novelty
- focus over parallel chaos
- validation before building
- constrained MVPs over oversized plans
- B2B before B2C unless evidence strongly supports another path
- clear ownership over vague collaboration

## Initial Projects

- Onboarding
- B2B - Product / Service / Company Ideation
- B2C - Product / Service / Company Ideation

## Near-Term Goal

Stand up the executive operating layer, identify the strongest initial B2B opportunity, and turn that into a clear validation path.

## Definition of Progress

Progress at SiAm.AI means:
- a decision was made
- ownership is clear
- next actions exist
- work is delegated to the right role
- the company is closer to a validated opportunity or working system

Thinking alone is not progress. Structured execution is.
